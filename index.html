Welcome

Caring For Pets

Comprehensive, Integrative Health Care for the Pets of Shawnee.

Companion animals are made up of so much more than just four legs and fur. They are complex, intelligent and uniquely special creatures. Shouldn’t their health care be just as exceptional?

At Best Friends Animal Clinic, we celebrate the uniqueness of each and every patient we serve. That’s why we offer such a comprehensive suite of services. From natural cures to conventional medicine, our “East meets West” approach ensures your pet will always receive the most precise, personalized care.

Since 1990, we’ve been proudly serving the people and pets of Shawnee & Bethel Acres providing a full range of traditional and integrative medicine options. More importantly, we deliver that care in a way that makes even the most nervous animal feel safe, comfortable and right at home.

Does your Shawnee vet provide this level of individualized care and attention? Are you looking for a second opinion or would like to explore more natural options for your pet? If so, we invite you to give Best Friends Animal Clinic a try.

From innovative treatment options to the ancient arts of acupuncture, herbs, essential oils and more, we’ve got you covered. Call or stop by any time. We can’t wait to meet you both!
